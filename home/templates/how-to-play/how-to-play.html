How to play

The objective of huberis is to get to the top level of the magic tower as fast as u can.

step 1: Select a card from your hand to play, increasing your stats for the next battle. after that, u discard one card.

step 2: The huberis phase, do you think u got what it takes to level up? Getting to the top of the tower as fast as u can is your goal, but dying means u go down a level and lose a chance to upgrade.

step 3: fight phase 1 to 4

In each phase u will be given the option to cast a spell or skip to the next phase. each spell has different phases in wich u can use them. And enemis have their own phases in wich they attack.

Keep an eye on your mana supply and plan your attacks carefully. You need to kill all enemies before the end of phase 4, not getting killed in the process....

step 4: Each kill gets u a certain number of xp, u get an extra card at 5 xp, 10xp, 15xp, 20xp. the counter is set to 0 when u recieve an extra card.

step 5: draw 2 new cards plus the ones u earned with the xp count. and reduce your hand to 8 cards.
