How to play

The objective of huberis is to get to the top level of the magic tower as fast as u can.

step 1: Select a card from your hand to play, increasing your stats for the next battle.

step 2: Fight phase 1 to 4

In each phase u will be given the option to cast a spell or skip to the next phase. each spell has different phases in wich u can use them. And enemys have their own phases in wich they attack.

Keep an eye on your mana supply and plan your attacks carefully. You need to kill all enemies before the end of phase 4, not getting killed in the process....

The attacks unavailable to you will be grey in the attackbar.
This can be either because the phase or your mana doesn't allow you to play this spell at the moment.
Or of course you haven't learned this spell at all..

step 3: Discard a card from your hand. You will draw 2 new cards after this.

step 4: The huberis phase, do you think u got what it takes to go up one level? Getting to the top of the tower as fast as u can is your goal.
But dying means u go down a level and u only have 6 playing cards to choose from.
